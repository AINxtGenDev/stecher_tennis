The Tennis Ranking List web application is used to manage tennis players and organize challenges between them.
In addition to displaying the current ranking list, the application offers functions for dynamically updating
the ranking positions and for managing player blocking and availability.

Backend: Python with the Flask framework
Real-time communication: Flask-SocketIO
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (jQuery, Bootstrap)
Database: SQLite
JSON data for initializing the players (initial_players.json)

Please always ask questions if something is unclear.

If players are in a challenge, they may not be asked to move up.
Examples:
Row 7 (e.g. rank 25) may normally challenge 24, 23, 22, 21, 20 and 19 (max. 6 places upwards). If, for example, rank 19 and rank 23
are currently in a challenge, a valid challenge may only be allowed between ranks 24, 22, 21, and 20, it may not be challenged further upwards.

Row 8 (e.g. rank 33) may normally challenge 32, 31, 30, 29, 28, 27, and 26 (max. 7 places upwards). If, for example, rank 26 and rank 31
are currently in a challenge and rank 28 is not available, challenges between the following ranks may be allowed.
Ranks 32, 30, 29, 27 and 25
