### **3. Challenge Logic**

   #### **For Top 10 players (Ranks 1–10):**  
   - Players can challenge any player ranked ahead of them (e.g., Rank 5 can challenge Ranks 1–4).

   #### **For Ranks 11–36:**  
   - Players in each row can challenge players within a defined range of ranks above them:
     | Row  | Ranks   | Max. Positions Upward |
     |------|---------|------------------------|
     | 5    | 11–15   | 4 positions            |
     | 6    | 16–21   | 5 positions            |
     | 7    | 22–28   | 6 positions            |
     | 8    | 29–36   | 7 positions            |

   #### **Unavailable Players:**  
   - If a player within the eligible challenge range is marked as unavailable, they are removed from the list of challengers.  
   - For each unavailable player, one additional rank above the normal challenge range is allowed.

   #### **New Players:**  
   - A player not currently in the ranking can challenge up to Rank 11.  
   - If they win, they take that rank, and the current player at that rank is pushed down.  
   - If they lose, they take the last position (Rank 36).

### **4. Availability Management**  
   - Players can be toggled between available and unavailable by clicking on their position in the pyramid.  
   - Display unavailable players along with a timestamp in the format `YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS`. Example:

     Unavailable Players:
     Noah Meyer         unavailable since 2025-02-11 11:53
     Elias Neumann      unavailable since 2025-02-18 15:53

### **5. Active Challenges**
   - Display active challenges with the challenger, opponent, and deadline in the format:

     Active Challenges:
     Finn Wolf     vs Noah Meyer      (Deadline: 2025-02-18 15:49)
     David Richter vs Paul Wagner     (Deadline: 2025-02-18 15:51)

### **6. Result Processing**
   - After a result is confirmed (challenger wins, opponent wins, or `not happened`), update the ranking pyramid:  
     - If the challenger wins:  
       - The challenger takes the opponent’s rank.  
       - The opponent and all players between the two shift down by one rank.  
     - If the challenger loses:  
       - The challenger keeps their rank.  

   - **Challenge Restrictions:**  
     - The winner can issue new challenges immediately but cannot be challenged for 7 days.  
     - The loser can be challenged immediately but cannot issue new challenges for 7 days.
